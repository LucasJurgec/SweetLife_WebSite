About Us

Sweet Life is a local, family-owned ice cream shop
with two locations: Brunswick and Williamstown.
We've been serving the Brunswick community for nearly 20 years,
and have recently expanded our ice cream delights to Williamstown.
We look forward to continuing to provide delicious and affordable
ice cream for everyone to enjoy.
